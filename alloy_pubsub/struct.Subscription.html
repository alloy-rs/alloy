Struct alloy_pubsub::Subscription

pub struct Subscription<T> {
    pub(crate) inner: RawSubscription,
    _pd: PhantomData<T>,
}
Expand description

A Subscription is a feed of notifications from the server of a specific type T, identified by a local ID.

For flexibility, we expose three similar APIs:

  • The Subscription::recv method and its variants will discard any notifications of unexpected types.
  • The Subscription::recv_any and its variants will yield unexpected types as SubscriptionItem::Other.
  • The Subscription::recv_result and its variants will attempt to deserialize the notifications and yield the serde_json::Result of the deserialization.
